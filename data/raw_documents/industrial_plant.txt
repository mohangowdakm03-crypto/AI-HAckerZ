INDUSTRIAL PLANT TOPOLOGY & OPERATIONS REPORT
Document ID: ET-HACKATHON-2026-DEMO

1.0 EQUIPMENT OVERVIEW
The main production line relies heavily on PUMP-101A and PUMP-101B. 
PUMP-101A is CONNECTED_TO the HEAT-EXCHANGER-01.
PUMP-101B serves as a redundant backup.
VALVE-204 regulates the flow from HEAT-EXCHANGER-01 to the PRIMARY-CENTRIFUGE.

2.0 SENSORS AND MONITORING
TEMP-SENS-08 actively MONITORS the HEAT-EXCHANGER-01. 
PRESSURE-SENS-12 actively MONITORS PUMP-101A.

3.0 HAZARDS AND RISKS
If PUMP-101A fails, it CAUSES a severe OVERHEATING-HAZARD in the system.
If the OVERHEATING-HAZARD occurs, it disrupts the entire COOLING-SYSTEM.

4.0 SAFETY PROCEDURES
To mitigate the OVERHEATING-HAZARD, operators must execute the EMERGENCY-COOLING-PROCEDURE. 
This procedure requires shutting down VALVE-204 and activating the backup PUMP-101B.
