The recent inspection of the WATER-COOLING-SYSTEM on July 20th revealed several emerging issues.
VALVE-204, which regulates the flow to HEAT-EXCHANGER-01, was found to be sticking due to mineral buildup. This restricts the water flow and causes secondary stress on PUMP-101B.
Furthermore, if PUMP-101B fails, it will trigger an immediate HIGH-PRESSURE-ALARM. We recommend replacing VALVE-204 to mitigate the risk of SYSTEM-OVERPRESSURE.
Additionally, the newly installed PRESSURE-SENS-12 is actively monitoring the output of PUMP-101B and is connected to the central SCADA-DASHBOARD.
