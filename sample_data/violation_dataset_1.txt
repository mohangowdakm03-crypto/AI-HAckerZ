SAFETY AUDIT: SECTOR 4
Date: Sept 1, 2026

The new HIGH-PRESSURE-BOILER was installed last week in Sector 4. 
It is a critical piece of equipment that provides steam to the entire facility.
Currently, the HIGH-PRESSURE-BOILER operates completely manually. 
If the pressure exceeds the limit, it CAUSES a CATASTROPHIC-EXPLOSION-HAZARD. 
Because of budget cuts, we have not installed any temperature or pressure sensors on this unit yet. 
Furthermore, there is no emergency shutdown protocol or mitigation step documented for the CATASTROPHIC-EXPLOSION-HAZARD.
